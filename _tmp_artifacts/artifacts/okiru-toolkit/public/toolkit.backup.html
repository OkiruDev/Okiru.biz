How to use this — in 5 simple steps

Brand new to AI? Follow these and you'll write better prompts than 90% of users on day one.

01
Pick your AI tool.
Choose ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, Perplexity, or Meta AI — whichever you have access to.
02
Describe your goal in plain words.
Type what you want — like writing to a smart colleague. Be specific about the result you need.
03
Hit Generate prompt.
The advisor fills in all six tiles — Persona, Exemplar, Format, Task, Tone, Context — for you.
04
Tweak any tile.
Edit any tile to match your exact needs. The final prompt updates live as you type.
05
Copy and paste into your AI.
Click Copy final prompt, then Open AI, and paste. Done — instant pro-level results.
