Questions

Starting out? Start here.

I have an idea but don't know where to start.
That is exactly who we build for. On your discovery call we turn your idea into a plan, your website, your offers, how you get paid and how you find clients, then we build it for you.
I've run my business off WhatsApp and a notebook. Where do I begin?
Start with a demo. We take what you already do, quotes, bookings, follow-ups, and put it into one tidy system, so you stop losing track and look professional overnight.
I'm not techy at all. Will I cope?
Yes. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use BizBrain. We set everything up for you and it asks plain questions, no jargon, no coding, ever.
What do I actually get for R999 a month?
A custom website, your own domain and business email, plus the tools to run leads, invoicing, bookings and social, all set up, maintained and improving over time.
I don't have a logo, domain or business email yet.
That is fine, we sort all of it. Domain and business email set-up are included, and we design around your brand, or help you shape one from scratch.
How long until I'm up and running?
Most businesses go live within a couple of weeks of the discovery call, depending on how much of your content and detail is ready to go.
Do I need a registered business first?
No. Side hustles are welcome. You can add your company registration and VAT details later if and when you formalise.
Is there a big upfront build fee?
No. Set-up, customisation and maintenance are all included in the monthly package, there is no large once-off bill just to get started.
What if my business grows or changes?
It grows with you. Small changes and extra features use R100 credit bundles of 1000 credits each; bigger builds are quoted. You are never stuck with a site you've outgrown.
What do I need to bring to get started?
Just your business details: what you sell, who your customers are and how you want to be paid. We build BizBrain around that and it learns as you go.
How is this different from Wix, QuickBooks or Zoho?
Those are separate tools you must connect and run yourself. BizBrain is one system where your website, leads, invoices and social already work together, set up and looked after for you.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Month to month, no contract, no cancellation fee. If you leave, you can export your clients, invoices and content first.
What about my data and POPIA?
Your data stays yours, stored securely and used only to run your tools. It is POPIA-compliant, and you can export or delete it at any time.
Is there a free trial?
We do a demo instead, because each BizBrain is custom built. We show you exactly what yours would look like before you commit to anything.
