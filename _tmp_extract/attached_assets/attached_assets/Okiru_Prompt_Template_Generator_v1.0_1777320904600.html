How to Use

Five steps to a perfect prompt every time.

01

Select your AI tool from the toggle at the top — ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, Perplexity, or Meta AI.

02

Type your goal in plain language. Describe what you want the AI to produce and be specific about the output you need.

03

Click Generate tiles. The toolkit populates Persona, Exemplar, Format, Task, Tone, and Context based on your goal.

04

Edit any tile to match your exact needs. The final prompt updates live as you type so you always see the finished result.

05

Click Copy final prompt, then click Open AI to go straight to your chosen tool and paste.

For a fully connected auto-generation experience, an API-backed Custom GPT is required. This static version works for everyone on free accounts and is ideal for class use.
